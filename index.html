VSYNK

We sync multiple screens

VSYNK is a video synchronization application built with openframeworks 0.74 with OS X 10.7 SDK. Currently, VSYNK runs only on Mac OS X. There will be Windows version in the future releases. You can download and install VSYNK software for free. Free version provides video synchronization up to two machines.

MANUAL

     Create Network on your master device. Wait a few seconds in order to your master machine get self-assigned ip. You can check your master device's ip from network preferences...Do nothing until you get an ip. Otherwise synchronization fails...

     Connect to the network, the one you created previous step, from your slave devices

     Run VSYNK-M.app on your master device

     Check your IP Address on Master Device. IP Address should be xxx.xxx.255.255 or xxx.xxx.0.255. If it is not, close and relaunch VSYNK-M.app.

     Drag & Drop your desired video ﬁle (uncompressed HD video ﬁles are suggested...) onto VSYNK-M.app on your master device. Video ﬁle plays automatically. By default, there is a footage video for testing purposes.

     Go to your slave devices. Run VSYNK.app on all of your slave devices. Drag & Drop your desired video ﬁle onto VSYNK.app. Video ﬁle plays automatically.
